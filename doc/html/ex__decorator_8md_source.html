1 # How to decorate classes to add functionalities {#decorate-classes}
2 
3 The [_Decroator patter_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decorator_pattern) is
4 extensively used within the library to dynamically add new functionality to other classes.
5 This is particularly useful to design flexible and reusable object-oriented software
6 providing an alternative to subclassing.
7 In particular, when there are **several independent ways of extending functionality**
8 and it is difficult to predict, at design time, what combinations of such
9 functionalities will be needed, subclassing would require to make every possible
10 (or most of the) combination of such functionalities, while **decorators provide
11 much more flexibility combining functionalities on a per-use basis and foster
12 code reuse**.<br>
